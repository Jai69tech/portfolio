Johnnyâ€™s Rampage is a top-down shooter developed as my final-year project. The purpose of this project is to demonstrate the fun factor of a top-down shooter by offering a variety of weapons for players to choose from in order to defeat enemies.

This game marks my first attempt at implementing AI and a weapon system. For the AI, I utilized a NavMesh to control enemy behaviors such as patrolling, walking, and chasing.

Regarding the weapon system, I implemented shooting mechanics using raycasting. I designed a flexible script that allows for easy modification of variables, enabling it to support different weapons with distinct attributes such as magazine capacity and reload time.
